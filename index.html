What The Bot Offers

VC Notifier Bot can offer a lot to your Discord server. Here are a few notable things the bot provides.

Blazing Fast Notifications

Notifications come in pretty much instantly, so with VC Notifier Bot you can be sure users are notified as soon as someone enters a voice channel.

Higher VC Engagement

By notifying users when someone joins a voice channel, people in every server are able to better engage with voice channels when other people are on too.

Reliable Uptime

At the time of writing, VC Notifier has a 95%+ uptime rate. You can depend on VC Notifier to be online and working when you want it to be.

Bot Commands

VC Notifier Bot has several useful commands. Here's an overview:

  • To get help, send a message with vc!help on any channel.
  • To change the channel in which VC Notifications appear, send a message with vc!sendnotificationshere on that channel.
  • To receive VC notifications for joins and leaves, send a message with vc!notifyme on any channel.
  • To stop receiving VC notifications for joins and leaves, send a message with vc!dontnotifyme on any channel.
  • To receive VC notifications for only joins, send a message with vc!notifymejoins on any channel.
  • To stop receiving VC notifications for only joins, send a message with vc!dontnotifymejoins on any channel.
